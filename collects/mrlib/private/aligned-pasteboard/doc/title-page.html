Abstract
The horizontal-panel% and the vertical-panel% classes provide simple interfaces to geometry management widgets. They do not, however, provide the robust set of features provided by the pasteboard% class such as scroll bars, file management, and undo/redo. The purpose of the horizontal-pasteboard% and vertical-pasteboard% class is to provide the power of the pasteboards coupled with simple geometry management found in panels optimized for insertion, removal, and retreval of snips.
