
(scheme-expand expr [attr 'previous] [vocab #f]) - expands one expression

(scheme-expand-program exprs [attr 'previous] [vocab #f]) - expands
  several expressions

elaboration-evaluator - parameter for the evaluatotr used to evaluate
 begin-elaboration-time bodies and the RHS of a macro definition

 default: (lambda (expr parsed->raw phase)
            (eval (parsed->raw expr)))

user-macro-body-evaluator - parameter for the evaluator used to
 evaluate macro applications

 default: (lambda (x . args)
            (eval `(,x ,@(map (lambda (x) `(#%quote ,x)) args))))
