DrScheme

> (drscheme:frame:basics-mixin a-fw:frame:standard-menus<%>)

  Extends input class with definitions for several of the standard DrScheme
  menus. It overrides: help-menu:after-about, file-menu:new,
  file-menu:between-new-and-open, file-menu:open, file-menu:about,
  file-menu:about-string.
